My article in LOAD magazine

Just a quick note to pass the time until the next full article: I made a contribution to last year’s LOAD magazine. You can find the download link for issue 7 here:

https://www.classic-computing.org/load-online/

An update should be coming soon (sooner than 2.5 years for sure ;) ).
